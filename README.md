#  ITC Hotels Revenue Optimization Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on building an interactive, multi-page Power BI dashboard for ITC Hotels to analyze financial performance, occupancy trends, booking behavior, and cancellations.

The objective is to transform raw hospitality data into actionable insights that help optimize revenue, improve occupancy, and reduce losses due to cancellations.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
The dashboard is designed to:
- Monitor revenue performance across hotels and room categories
- Analyze occupancy and capacity utilization
- Understand booking behavior and customer patterns
- Identify revenue leakage due to cancellations
- Enable data-driven decision-making for stakeholders

---

## 🗂️ Data Scope
The dataset includes:
- Hotel-level revenue data
- Room category details
- Booking and check-in information
- Occupancy and capacity metrics
- Cancellation records

---

## ⚙️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
  - Data Modeling (Star Schema)
  - DAX (Calculated Measures & KPIs)
  - Interactive Visualizations
  - Slicers & Filters
- Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

### 📍 Page 1: Financial Overview & Revenue Performance
**Key Metrics:**
- Total Revenue
- Cumulative Revenue
- MoM & WoW Growth
- Moving Average of Revenue
- Average Daily Rate (ADR)
- Revenue per Available Room (RevPAR)

**Insights Delivered:**
- Revenue trends across hotels and room categories
- Growth patterns over time
- Performance benchmarking using ADR & RevPAR

---

### 📍 Page 2: Occupancy & Capacity Analysis
**Key Metrics:**
- Occupancy Rate
- Occupancy Growth (MoM & WoW)

**Insights Delivered:**
- Capacity utilization across hotels and categories
- Seasonal and time-based occupancy patterns
- Correlation between occupancy and revenue

---

### 📍 Page 3: Room Category Performance & Booking Insights
**Key Metrics:**
- Revenue by Room Category
- Average Length of Stay (ALOS)
- Booking Lead Time

**Insights Delivered:**
- High-performing room categories
- Booking behavior trends
- Impact of lead time on revenue and occupancy

---

### 📍 Page 4: Cancellations & Lost Revenue Analysis
**Key Metrics:**
- Cancellation Rate
- Cumulative Cancellations
- Lost Revenue due to Cancellations
- Cancellation Growth (WoW)

**Insights Delivered:**
- Financial impact of cancellations
- Patterns in cancellation behavior
- Identification of high-risk segments

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights (Customize if possible)
- Certain room categories contribute disproportionately to revenue
- Higher occupancy does not always translate to higher revenue (impact of pricing)
- Cancellation rates significantly affect overall profitability
- Booking lead time plays a critical role in occupancy optimization

## 📸 Dashboard Features
- Multi-page interactive dashboard
- Dynamic filtering using slicers
- Time-based trend analysis (daily, weekly, monthly)
- KPI cards for quick performance tracking
- Drill-down capabilities for deeper insights
